Scrabble Game

Build words, score points, beat your high score.

How to Play

Quick steps
  • Drag tiles from the rack onto the board.
  • Tiles should form a connected word (no gaps).
  • Click Validate Word to check the word and score it.
  • Use Refresh Letters to get a new rack.
  • Use Clear Board to return tiles to the rack.
  • Use Start Over to reset everything.

Rules

Show rules
  • Words must be at least 2 letters.
  • Bonus squares can multiply your score.
  • Double-word squares multiply the total word score by 2.
  • Once a word is validated, repeating it won’t score again.
  • Blank tiles can be used as any letter (0 points).
